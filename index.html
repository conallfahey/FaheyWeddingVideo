FAQ

Do you record vows and speeches?

Yes! We use professional lavalier microphones and backup recorders to ensure crystal clear audio of your vows and toasts.

How soon is the film delivered?

We take great care in editing your film. Typical delivery time is within 3-4 weeks after your wedding day.
